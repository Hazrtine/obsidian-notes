	<span class="sub-sub-header">Rogerian</span>
	<div class="separation-box">
		<li>Claim: Main point of thesis.</li>
		<li>Grounds: Support for your claim; facts, evidence, statistics, expert advice, etc.</li>
		<li>Warrant: The reasoning that connects Grounds to Claim.</li>
		<li>Backing: Additional support for the warrant.</li>
		<li>Qualifier: Indicates strength of claim with words like "most likely," "usually," "in many cases," etc.</li>
		<li>Rebuttal: Acknowledge and respond to counterarguments and show why your claim still stands despite objections.</li>
